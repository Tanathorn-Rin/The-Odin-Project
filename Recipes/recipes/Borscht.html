Borscht

Ingredients:

  • 1 large beet, peeled and grated
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 carrot, peeled and grated
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 small cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream and fresh dill for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the beef broth to a boil.
  2. Add the beets, potatoes, and carrot. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Add the onion and cabbage. Simmer for another 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste and garlic. Simmer for 10 more minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, topped with a dollop of sour cream and fresh dill.
