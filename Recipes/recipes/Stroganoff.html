Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked egg noodles for serving
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their juices.
  4. Add the beef slices and cook until browned.
  5. Sprinkle flour over the mixture and stir well.
  6. Gradually add the beef broth, stirring constantly until thickened.
  7. Reduce heat and stir in the sour cream. Simmer gently for a few minutes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve over cooked egg noodles, garnished with fresh parsley.
