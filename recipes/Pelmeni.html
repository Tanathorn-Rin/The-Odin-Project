Pelmeni

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the water and egg.
  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth and elastic. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/8 inch thick. Cut into small circles using a glass or cookie cutter.
  7. Place a small amount of filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold the dough over the filling and pinch the edges to seal.
  8. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pelmeni and cook until they float to the surface, about 5-7 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with sour cream.
